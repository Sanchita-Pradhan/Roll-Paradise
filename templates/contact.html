Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What are your delivery areas?

We deliver within a 10-mile radius of our restaurant. Delivery fees vary by distance, starting at ₹50 for orders within 3 miles. Free delivery on orders over ₹1000!

Do you offer catering for events?

Yes! We offer catering services for events of all sizes. Our catering menu includes platters, individual wraps, and custom options. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Can I customize my roll?

Absolutely! You can build your own roll with our interactive customization tool. Choose from over 20 fresh ingredients and create your perfect combination.

What if I have food allergies?

We take food allergies seriously. All our ingredients are clearly labeled, and our staff is trained to handle special dietary requirements. Please inform us of any allergies.

How do I track my order?

Once you place an order, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order in real-time through our mobile app.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes! We have a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan options. Our Mediterranean Veggie and Tandoori Paneer rolls are customer favorites among vegetarians.
